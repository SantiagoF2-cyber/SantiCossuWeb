About Me

Hi, I'm Santiago Cossu, a web developer focused on static code and front-end development. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to create modern, fast websites that work well on any device.

I like to work in a clear and detail-oriented way. I stay attentive to every step of the process and maintain constant communication to ensure the final result is exactly what the client needs (or even better).

I always meet the agreed deadlines, and I can join a project on an hourly basis, for a full project, or through milestone-based work, depending on what the project requires. My goal is to keep everything simple, organized, and focused on delivering a solution that truly works for the user.
